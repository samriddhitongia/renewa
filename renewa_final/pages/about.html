FAQ

Common Questions

How is energy actually delivered?
Energy is delivered via the existing electrical grid. Renewa issues digital Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs) that prove your consumption was matched to a verified renewable source — similar to carbon offsets but for electricity.
What energy types can be listed?
Solar PV, Wind turbines, and Biomass/Biogas are currently supported. Hydroelectric and geothermal support is coming Q3 2026. All sources must meet our green certification standards before listing.
How are prices determined?
Producers set their own prices. Our AI recommends optimal pricing based on local demand, time of day, energy type and market conditions. Typical Renewa prices are 20–35% below utility rates.
Are investments protected?
All projects are vetted by our investment team with binding offtake agreements, verified land rights and engineering reports. We offer limited escrow protection for retail investors under $50,000.
What are Renewa's fees?
We charge a 2.5% transaction fee on energy purchases, split between buyer and seller. Listing is always free. For investment projects, a 1% management fee applies on returns — only when you profit.
